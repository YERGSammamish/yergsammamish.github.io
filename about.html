About YERG

We are an environmental restoration organization composed of young people who have a passion to better the world for people and the other organisms that inhabit it. We do work spanning from the simplest litter cleanups, to complete restoration and rewilding of deprived areas. We not only do hands- on work in the field of habitat conservation, but we also reach and expose youth around us to the environmental issues that we face, through our social media and blog.
